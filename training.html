Exploit Reversing

Training

Duration: 2 days
Exploits are a key part of modern cyberattacks and are often so complex that only specialists can understand them. They pose the biggest threat to a corporate network, often being a kingdom gate keys. As exploits become more common and easier to access, partly due to automation and AI-powered research, the need to analyze and understand these threats increases. In our training, we share techniques of exploit analysis and insights we've gained from years of examining sophisticated threats.
Outline:
  • Exploit analysis techniques
  • Identification and extraction of stages
  • Shellcode analysis: 32/64 x86, ARM64 code
  • Using kernel debugging
  • Debugging and instrumentation on a physical device
  • Identification of the exploit target software versions
  • Effective exploit detection
