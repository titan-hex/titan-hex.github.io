Threat sinkholing

Project

Have you ever come across network indicators of compromise, like IP addresses and domains? Wondering how to utilize them beyond mere blacklisting or monitoring within your infrastructure? Here's how we can assist. Beyond the usual strategies, we can initiate takedown requests or manage sinkholes, keeping these indicators active. This strategy allows us to identify additional attack targets, enabling you to craft a more effective, coordinated response to ongoing threats. Operating such an infrastructure is typically reserved for security researchers due to the necessity to handle abuse reports, law enforcement requests, and ensure proper management to continue gathering crucial threat intelligence.

Threat deception

Project

Our innovative threat deception solution enhances your security measures with minimal effort. It includes the capability to generate decoy files and deploy network sensors, operating without the need for installation, thanks to its agentless design. This makes it highly effective in detecting insider threats and preventing data leaks. It's a standalone solution that integrates easily with your current systems.
