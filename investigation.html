Evidence acquisition

Solid clues

Collecting all evidence related to an attacker's activities is key. You might need it immediately to make important decisions during disaster response, or, it could be vital later for legal matters with law enforcement or compliance bodies. In any case, it's crucial to carefully collect, describe, and document all your findings and data samples. From assisting law enforcement to first-hand experience of working with INTERPOL, we have a deep understanding of how forensic procedures work and we can assist you in managing this critical process smoothly and efficiently.
Timeline analysis

Time matters

One of the most practical uses of forensic expertise lies in reconstructing the timeline of events. Knowing when an incident started, who was involved, and how it connects to other events is important. However, assembling an accurate timeline is complex as it requires analysing a lot of data in different formats. This is one of our specialties. Our reports include carefully crafted timelines of security events, giving you a clear understanding of what happened. Let us help make this complex process easier for you.
Forensic artefact drilldown

Dig deeper

Identifying malicious software is just the beginning. Analysing attackers' tools and techniques they employ is crucial for grasping their motives, level of expertise and tactics. This analysis often involves a complex process of reverse engineering and skill assessment - skills that are honed through years of experience in the field. Let our team's seasoned expertise, demonstrated by successfully deciphering code from novice cybercriminals to professional mercenaries and state-sponsored intelligence services, assist you in effectively understanding and combating cyber threats.
Continuous security sweep

Recurring action

One security check may not fully protect your systems. That's why we guide you through a careful process of ongoing monitoring and gathering attack evidence. We help by creating custom detection tools and offering additional clues about potential threats. This strategy is designed to help you gain a comprehensive understanding of the events, ensuring no detail is overlooked. You can rely on us to help you stay secure and maintain a robust defense against ongoing cyber threats.
Data leaks discovery

Exposed info

Security concerns can arise from various sources, not just network breaches. They could stem from insider data leaks, intercepted communication, or vulnerabilities in third-party services. Our team is adept at identifying the extent to which your assets or credentials have been compromised and circulated on illegal marketplaces and hacking forums.This information is vital for understanding if a breach claim is linked to a recent incident or an older issue. Allow us to help you navigate and clarify the security challenges your organization faces.
