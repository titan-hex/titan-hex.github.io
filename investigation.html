Evidence acquisition

Solid clues

Collecting all evidence related to an attacker's activities is key. You might need this information right away to help make important decisions and steer the direction of your incident response. Or, it could become important later on during legal matters with law enforcement or compliance bodies. In any case, it's important to carefully gather, describe, and document all your findings and sample data. We have a deep understanding of how forensic procedures work and are here to offer our support. Let us assist you in managing this critical process smoothly and efficiently.
Timeline analysis

Time matters

One of the most practical uses of forensic expertise lies in reconstructing the timeline of events. Understanding the origins of an incident, the number of people involved, and its overlap with other events can be crucial. However, assembling an accurate timeline is complex, involving the analysis of vast amounts of data in various formats. This is our specialty. Our reports feature meticulously constructed timelines of security events, offering you a clear picture of the situation. Let our expertise simplify this intricate process for you.

Data leaks discovery

Exposed info

A security compromise isn't always linked to a network breach. It could result from an insider data leak, interception of communication channels, or vulnerabilities in services provided by a third party. We specialize in verifying these situations. Our team is adept at identifying the extent to which your assets or credentials have been compromised and circulated on illegal marketplaces and hacking forums. This insight is crucial for determining if a breach claim is connected to a recent security incident or if it relates to an older, previously unresolved issue. Allow us to help you navigate and clarify the security challenges your organization faces.
