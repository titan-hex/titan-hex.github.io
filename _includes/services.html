How

Understanding the mechanics of an attack is crucial. Our clients frequently inquire about this first. We delve into the nature of the cyberattack, its underlying reasons, and critical technical elements, to create effective solutions. Our specialty is in unraveling these complex operations for a thorough understanding and robust countermeasures.
02

When

How long has the breach been ongoing? Determining the duration of a cyberattack is a critical aspect of any investigation. Through a retrospective analysis, we collect and assemble evidence to reveal the full extent of the incident. We help to identify when the breach initiated, how it evolved, and the steps taken by the attackers.
03

Why

Why did the attack happen? Was it driven by unbridled curiosity, financial interests, reconnaissance efforts, or an aggressive denial-of-service attack? Understanding the motivation behind a cyber attack is crucial. We are equipped to help you uncover and comprehend these underlying reasons.
04

Who

The final and most significant question in our investigations is: Who is responsible? Identifying the perpetrator requires extensive time and effort, as we meticulously collect every piece of information and carefully rule out any false leads. We provide you with clear pointers and traces leading to the identity of the culprits.
